# Quick Access Guide - AI Scanner on Dashboard

## 🎯 Quick Summary

The AI Scanner feature is now available on the User Dashboard, positioned between "My Prescriptions" and "Recent Orders" for easy access.

## 🚀 How to Access (3 Ways)

### Method 1: From Dashboard (NEW!)
1. Login to your account
2. Go to Dashboard (click your email in header)
3. Scroll to "Specialized AI Features" section
4. Click "Start AI Scanning →"

### Method 2: From Navigation Menu
1. Login to your account
2. Click "AI Scanner" in the top navigation
3. Start scanning

### Method 3: Direct URL
```
http://localhost:3000/medicine-scanner
```

## 🎯 Two Scanner Options

### 1. AI Strip Scanner (Left Card)
- **Purpose**: Scan medicine strip/blister pack photos
- **Technology**: AI + OCR
- **Output**: Detected medicines with confidence scores
- **Action**: Adds to cart automatically
- **Best For**: Elderly users, quick reorders

### 2. Rx Expert Scanner (Right Card)
- **Purpose**: Scan handwritten prescriptions
- **Technology**: Advanced OCR
- **Output**: Extracted medicine details
- **Action**: Matches medicines in database
- **Best For**: New prescriptions, doctor's orders

## 📱 Mobile Access

On mobile devices:

## ⚡ Quick Tips

### For Best Results
- Use good lighting
- Keep medicine strip flat
- Take photo from directly above
- Ensure text is clearly visible

### Common Use Cases
- **Reordering**: Scan your current medicine strip
- **New Prescription**: Scan doctor's prescription
- **Elderly Care**: Help elderly family members order medicines
- **Quick Shopping**: Skip manual search, just scan

## 🎓 First Time Users

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Login**: Use your credentials
2. **Navigate**: Go to Dashboard
3. **Locate**: Find "Specialized AI Features" section
4. **Choose**: Pick AI Strip Scanner or Rx Expert Scanner
5. **Upload**: Select your medicine/prescription image
6. **Scan**: Click the scan button
7. **Review**: Check detected medicines
8. **Add**: Add to cart
9. **Checkout**: Complete your order

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Maximize Efficiency
- Save frequently used medicines to favorites
- Use AI Scanner for quick reorders
- Keep prescription images in your phone
- Scan multiple medicines at once

### Troubleshooting
- **No detection**: Retake photo with better lighting
- **Wrong medicine**: Check image quality
- **Slow scan**: Reduce image size
- **Upload failed**: Check internet connection
