# Module 5 - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Quick Start - OTP Delivery Verification

### For Delivery Personnel

#### Step 1: Mark Order as Picked Up
1. Login to Delivery Dashboard
2. Go to "Assigned Orders" tab
3. Find your assigned order
4. Click "Mark as Picked Up" button
5. ✅ Customer receives OTP email automatically

#### Step 2: Deliver to Customer
1. Navigate to customer location
2. Arrive at delivery address
3. Ask customer: "May I have your delivery OTP please?"
4. Customer will check their email and provide 6-digit code

#### Step 3: Verify OTP
1. In Delivery Dashboard, click "OTP Verification" tab
2. Select the order from dropdown
3. Enter the 6-digit OTP customer provided
4. Click "Confirm Delivery" button
5. ✅ See success message: "Order marked as delivered successfully"

### For Customers

#### What to Expect:
1. You'll receive an email when delivery boy picks up your order
2. Email contains your unique 6-digit OTP
3. Keep this OTP ready
4. When delivery boy arrives, share the OTP
5. Delivery boy enters OTP to confirm delivery
6. ✅ Your order is securely delivered!

### Sample OTP Email

```
Subject: Order Status Update - #ORD123

Hello, John Doe!

This OTP ensures secure delivery and prevents wrong 
product handover.
```

## 🎯 Key Points

### Security:
- ✅ Never share OTP with anyone except delivery person
- ✅ OTP is unique for each order
- ✅ OTP required for delivery confirmation

### Delivery Boy:
- ✅ Always ask customer for OTP
- ✅ Enter OTP in "OTP Verification" tab
- ✅ Wait for success notification
- ✅ Do not mark as delivered without OTP

### Customer:
- ✅ Check email for OTP after order is picked up
- ✅ Keep OTP ready when delivery arrives
- ✅ Share OTP only with delivery person
- ✅ Verify product before sharing OTP

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

### "OTP Email Not Received"
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait 1-2 minutes for email delivery
- Contact support if still not received

### "Invalid OTP Code"
- Double-check the 6 digits
- Ensure no spaces or extra characters
- Ask customer to read OTP again
- Verify you selected correct order

### "Success Notification Not Showing"
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Try again after a few seconds
- Contact support if issue persists
