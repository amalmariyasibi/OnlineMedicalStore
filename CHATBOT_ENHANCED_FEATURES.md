## 🔍 Smart Symptom Analysis

### Multi-Symptom Recognition
```
User: "I have fever, headache, and body ache"
Bot: Recognizes all three symptoms
     Provides medicines for each
     Adds relevant tips for flu-like symptoms
```

### Contextual Advice
Based on symptoms detected, the bot adds specific tips:

**For Fever/Headache/Body ache:**
- Get plenty of rest
- Stay hydrated
- Monitor temperature

**For Cough/Cold/Sore throat:**
- Drink warm liquids
- Use humidifier
- Avoid cold drinks

**For Stomach issues:**
- Eat light, bland foods
- Avoid spicy foods
- Smaller, frequent meals

**For Diarrhea/Vomiting:**
- Stay hydrated with ORS
- BRAT diet
- Avoid dairy temporarily

### Urgency Detection
- **Emergency**: Chest pain, difficulty breathing → Immediate ER
- **Moderate**: Persistent fever → Doctor within 24-48 hours
- **Low**: Mild headache → Self-care at home
